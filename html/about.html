Bio

I was born in Ypsilanti, Michigan in 1990. I don't like to share the day because I dislike birthdays, but if you're glad I was born that's great anytime.

I was a mediocre student who always impressed her teachers in other ways- the norm for me was to fail to turn in my homework, only for the teacher to give me enough partial credit for finishing all of my missing work in a mad dash before the semester ended. Turns out I have ADHD, who would have guessed. I also forgot to eat lunch or breakfast for several years of my childhood. Oops!

In college I was an art major, then a physics major, then finally a math major. I love what I learned but rarely get to use it-until now, that is. Programming feels like a natural place to exercise my logical chops.

I have struggled with my health for about a decade, but I was finally diagnosed with Crohn's disease in 2018 and got treatment for what really ailed me. I still don't have the energy I wish I had, but I have had barely any pain these last few months and I'm grateful to be able to say that. Living in a body is a lot of work.
